The aw:capture element captures the HTML generated by processing its contents.

Variables

The context map must contain the contents variable. When the aw:capture element is processed, the HTML fragment generated by processing its contents is appended to the value of this variable.

Example

Here is a sample use of aw:capture : 

<aw:noop newvar.contents="">
	<aw:capture>
		<b>Hello World!</b>
	</aw:capture>
	<p>
		Captured, twice encoded: <aw:contents name="contents" />
	</p>
	<p>
		Captured, once encoded: <aw:encodedContents name="contents" />
	</p>
</aw:noop>

And here is the result:
